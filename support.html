Rainbow Six CUBA Companion Support

What is this extension?

Rainbow Six CUBA Companion helps users synchronize public Rainbow Six Siege statistics from R6 Tracker with the Rainbow Six CUBA Discord community.

What does it do?

When you open your R6 Tracker profile through the Rainbow Six CUBA sync process, the extension reads the public statistics visible on that page and sends them to Rainbow Six CUBA so your server profile, rank, and competitive stats can be updated.

What does it not do?

It does not ask for Ubisoft passwords, Discord passwords, email credentials, payment information, or private messages.

How do I use it?

1. Install the extension.

2. Go to the Rainbow Six CUBA Discord server.

3. Use the Ubisoft/R6 Stats sync panel.

4. Open the R6 Tracker link generated by the bot.

5. The extension will sync supported public statistics automatically.

Troubleshooting

If your stats do not update:

  • Confirm the extension is installed and enabled.
  • Open the R6 Tracker link generated by the Discord bot.
  • Make sure the Ubisoft username is spelled correctly.
  • Try the sync again from the R6 Stats panel.
  • Contact Rainbow Six CUBA support if the issue continues.

Support contact

For support, open a ticket in the official Rainbow Six CUBA Discord server.
