Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

**Extension:** Rainbow Six CUBA Companion

**Developer/Publisher:** Rainbow Six CUBA

1. Purpose

Rainbow Six CUBA Companion is a browser extension created to help members of the Rainbow Six CUBA community voluntarily synchronize publicly visible Rainbow Six Siege profile information from R6 Tracker with Rainbow Six CUBA community systems.

The extension exists for one purpose: to support community features such as verified R6 profiles, rank visibility, server rankings, competitive statistics, recruitment tools, and community leaderboards.

2. Information We Collect

When a user chooses to synchronize a profile, the extension may collect and transmit the following information:

  • Ubisoft username associated with the R6 Tracker profile being opened.
  • Public Rainbow Six Siege statistics visible on R6 Tracker.
  • Public match, rank, operator, map, and season information visible on R6 Tracker.
  • Synchronization status, timestamps, and technical metadata needed to process the sync request.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

The extension does not collect, request, store, or transmit:

  • Ubisoft passwords.
  • Ubisoft authentication tokens.
  • Ubisoft cookies.
  • Discord passwords.
  • Email passwords.
  • Payment information.
  • Private messages.
  • Private Discord content.
  • General browsing history.
  • Content from websites unrelated to supported R6 Tracker pages.
  • Information from pages the user does not voluntarily open.

4. How the Extension Works

The extension only runs on supported R6 Tracker pages. When a user opens a supported R6 Tracker profile page as part of the Rainbow Six CUBA synchronization process, the extension reads the publicly visible profile information displayed on that page and sends the relevant statistics to Rainbow Six CUBA servers.

The extension does not log in to Ubisoft, does not bypass authentication, does not access private Ubisoft account data, and does not require the user to provide Ubisoft credentials.

5. How We Use Information

Information collected through the extension is used only for Rainbow Six CUBA community features, including:

  • Player profile synchronization.
  • Rank verification within the community.
  • Competitive statistics.
  • Community rankings and leaderboards.
  • Team recruitment and scouting tools.
  • Community moderation or administrative review when necessary.

We do not sell user data.

6. Data Sharing

Rainbow Six CUBA does not sell collected information to third parties. Data may be processed through Rainbow Six CUBA infrastructure and may be displayed inside Rainbow Six CUBA community systems when users choose to participate in statistics-based features.

Data may be disclosed if required by law, to protect against abuse, or to maintain the security and integrity of Rainbow Six CUBA services.

7. User Control

Use of the extension is voluntary. Users may stop using the extension at any time by uninstalling it from their browser.

Users may request removal of their synchronized Rainbow Six CUBA profile data through official Rainbow Six CUBA support channels.

8. Data Retention

Rainbow Six CUBA may retain synchronized statistics and related metadata for operational, historical, ranking, moderation, and community management purposes. Data may be updated, replaced, archived, or deleted as part of normal system operations.

9. Security

Rainbow Six CUBA applies reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect synchronized information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. Children and Minors

The extension is intended for users who are allowed to participate in Rainbow Six CUBA community features under the rules of the Discord server and applicable platform requirements. Users who are not eligible to participate should not use the extension.

11. Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with public pages from R6 Tracker and Rainbow Six CUBA services. Users should also review the privacy policies and terms of those services. Rainbow Six CUBA is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the extension after changes are posted means the user accepts the updated policy.

13. Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or profile removal requests, contact Rainbow Six CUBA through its official Discord server support system.
